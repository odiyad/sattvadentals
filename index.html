Dental FAQs

Yes, professional teeth whitening performed under dental supervision is safe and effective. We use clinically approved whitening agents and protective measures to ensure no damage to teeth or gums. Results are visible and long-lasting when combined with proper home care.

A typical root canal appointment lasts 60-90 minutes, depending on tooth complexity and access. We use advanced techniques and local anesthesia to ensure your comfort. Dr. Tanwar will discuss the timeline during your initial consultation.

Implants are an excellent long-term solution for missing teeth, offering durability and natural appearance. However, candidacy depends on bone density, gum health, and overall health. We'll evaluate your situation and discuss all options during a free consultation.

Tooth sensitivity has multiple causes‚Äîenamel erosion, gum recession, or decay. We'll diagnose the cause and recommend targeted solutions, which may include specialized toothpaste, protective treatments, or other interventions. Relief is typically achieved within a few weeks.

We provide detailed aftercare instructions tailored to your treatment. General recommendations include avoiding staining foods/drinks, maintaining rigorous oral hygiene, and regular checkups. Most cosmetic results last 5-10 years with proper care.

Hair Treatment FAQs

Hair loss has multiple causes‚Äîgenetics, hormonal changes, nutritional deficiencies, stress, and medical conditions. Many types are reversible or manageable with proper treatment. Dr. Sushmita diagnoses the underlying cause and recommends appropriate solutions. Early intervention improves outcomes significantly.

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) contains growth factors that stimulate dormant hair follicles. When injected into the scalp, PRP promotes blood flow to follicles and prolongs the growth phase. Results typically appear after 3-4 months, with continued improvement over time.

Yes, FDA-approved hair restoration medications have a strong safety profile with minimal side effects when used as directed. We monitor patients during treatment and adjust protocols if needed. Dr. Sushmita will discuss potential side effects and benefits during your consultation.

Results depend on the treatment and your commitment to maintenance. PRP typically requires maintenance sessions 1-2 times yearly after the initial course. Medications must be continued to maintain results. We'll outline a long-term maintenance plan to sustain your progress.

Absolutely. Dr. Sushmita often combines PRP with topical treatments and nutritional support for enhanced results. This multi-modal approach addresses hair loss from different angles, improving efficacy and speeding visible results.
